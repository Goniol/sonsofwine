The Project

Sons of Wine is a natural wine project founded by Farid Yahimi. The wines are produced from organic or biodynamic grapes sourced from different terroirs and vinified with minimal intervention.

Fermentation with indigenous yeasts, no filtration and minimal sulfur. Each cuvée is produced in small quantities.

Farid Yahimi

Farid Yahimi is a French natural wine maker originally from Lorraine. After a career in digital technology he turned toward natural wine and began producing experimental micro-cuvées under the label Sons of Wine.

The project is based on the idea of a free winemaker sourcing organic grapes and creating wines without technological manipulation.
